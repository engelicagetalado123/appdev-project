Barangay Assembly

Barangay residence & officials assembly

Date & Time:October 26, 2025 - 8:00AM - 5:00PM
Location:Barangay Covered Gym

Event Highlights:

The Barangay successfully conducted its Barangay Assembly this October 26, 2025 bringing together residents, local leaders, and stakeholders to discuss important community matters.

During the assembly, the barangay officials presented updates on completed and ongoing projects, financial reports, and upcoming programs aimed at improving public services and community welfare.
